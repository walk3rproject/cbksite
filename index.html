Our Story

WHYWE EXIST

Utah has thousands of young people aging out of foster care, battling addiction, and rebuilding after trauma — with no map and no one walking beside them. The Comeback Kid Project was built to be that presence. We show up. We don't leave. And we give youth the tools, mentors, and community they need to write the next chapter.

Our three core values drive everything we do:

Integrity — We are who we say we are, regardless of who is around.
Accountability — We keep our commitments. We take responsibility for our results.
Service — Healing comes through lifting others.
