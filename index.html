The Comeback Kid Project was built to be that presence. We show up. We don't leave. And we give youth the tools, mentors, and community they need to write the next chapter. Our founder, Michael Walker, knows what it's like to fight back from rock bottom. He battled addiction as a young man and built his own comeback — and then built the organization he wished existed when he needed it most. Our three core values drive everything we do: Integrity — We are who we say we are, regardless of who is around. Accountability — We keep our commitments. We take responsibility for our results. Service — Healing comes through lifting others.
