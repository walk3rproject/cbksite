COMMON QUESTIONS

How do I become a mentor?

Reach out via the form above or email us at info@cbkproject.org. We'll walk you through the process, including background checks and our trauma-informed training.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes. The Comeback Kid Project, Inc. is a registered 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN: 33-4594581). All donations are fully tax-deductible.

How can I refer a youth who needs help?

Email us or call (801) 901-8491. We'll have a confidential conversation about how we can help.

Where does CBK Project operate?

We serve youth across the Wasatch Front and Utah County, with our base of operations in Lehi, Utah.
