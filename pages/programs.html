CBK LAUNCH

Mentorship · Life Skills · Integrity

CBK Launch pairs each young person with a dedicated mentor who walks alongside them as they build an independent life. While we serve all youth facing adversity, we have a particular focus on teens aging out of the foster care system — young people who are expected to navigate adulthood with no family, no plan, and no safety net.

This isn't a handout. It's a hand up. Mentors help with everything from creating a plan to finding stability — housing, employment, education, and the daily grind of building a future.

Rooted in integrity — because we are who we say we are, regardless of who is around.

What CBK Launch Includes

  • ▸Matched one-on-one mentorship
  • ▸Life planning and goal setting
  • ▸Employment and education support
  • ▸Trauma-informed guidance
  • ▸Consistent, reliable connection

CBK WORKSHOPS

Real Skills · Real World · Accountability

Practical, hands-on workshops that build the skills no one teaches you in school — financial literacy, emotional regulation, job readiness, basic car and home maintenance, sports and recreation, and more.

Every workshop is paired with accountability — because real growth happens when you commit to showing up, doing the work, and owning the results.

Driven by accountability — we keep our commitments and take responsibility for our results.

Workshop Topics

  • ▸Financial literacy and budgeting
  • ▸Mental health and self-care tools
  • ▸Job readiness and interview skills
  • ▸Basic auto and home maintenance
  • ▸Sports, recreation & outdoor activities
  • ▸Leadership and communication

THE LOLLY PROJECT

Service · Community · Healing

The Lolly Project is our service arm — and the heart of everything CBK Project believes in. We serve the elderly, widowed, and disabled in our community by providing yard cleanups, yard maintenance, and other acts of service at no cost to those we serve.

Here's how it works: teens participate in service projects and in return, they earn service hours, a stipend for their work, and access to exclusive CBK Workshops and events. Many are also given tips by the people they serve — because when you show up with a good heart and a lawnmower, people notice.

But the real payoff isn't money. When a young person who has been through the worst finds themselves helping someone else, something shifts. Purpose replaces pain. Connection replaces isolation. That's the magic of service — and that's The Lolly Project.

Powered by service — because healing comes through lifting others.

How The Lolly Project Works

  • ▸Free yard cleanups & maintenance for elderly, widowed & disabled
  • ▸Teens earn service hours and a stipend for their work
  • ▸Access to CBK Workshops and exclusive events
  • ▸Real community impact — real healing through purpose
  • ▸Tips often given by grateful community members

WHERE THE NAME CAME FROM

"The name 'Lolly' came from my five-year-old son. He couldn't quite say 'Lonnie' — my grandma's name — so he called her Grandma Lolly. It stuck. I started doing her yard care for free, and as I served, I felt something I hadn't felt in a long time — love for the people I was helping, and the self-worth I'd been searching for. I decided that the way I felt happy was through serving others. The Lolly Project grew from that."

— Michael Walker, Founder
