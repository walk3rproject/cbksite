Our Programs

THREE WAYS
WE SHOW UP

Each program is built on one of our core values. Together, they create a complete foundation for young people to rebuild, grow, and thrive.

01

CBK LAUNCH

Mentorship · Life Skills · Integrity

For youth stepping out of foster care, trauma, or tough situations — CBK Launch pairs each young person with a dedicated mentor who walks alongside them as they build an independent life.

This isn't a handout. It's a hand up. Mentors help with everything from creating a plan to finding stability — housing, employment, education, and the daily grind of building a future.

Rooted in integrity — because we are who we say we are, regardless of who is around.

What CBK Launch Includes

  • ▸Matched one-on-one mentorship
  • ▸Life planning and goal setting
  • ▸Employment and education support
  • ▸Trauma-informed guidance
  • ▸Consistent, reliable connection

02

CBK WORKSHOPS

Real Skills · Real World · Accountability

Practical, hands-on workshops that build the skills no one teaches you in school — financial literacy, emotional regulation, job readiness, basic car and home maintenance, and more.

Every workshop is paired with accountability — because real growth happens when you commit to showing up, doing the work, and owning the results.

Driven by accountability — we keep our commitments and take responsibility for our results.

Workshop Topics

  • ▸Financial literacy and budgeting
  • ▸Mental health and self-care tools
  • ▸Job readiness and interview skills
  • ▸Basic auto and home maintenance
  • ▸Leadership and communication

03

THE LOLLY PROJECT

Service · Community · Healing

Youth turn their hardest experiences into their greatest strength — by serving others. The Lolly Project organizes community service for widows, the elderly, and neighbors in need.

When a young person who has been through the worst finds themselves helping someone else, something shifts. Purpose replaces pain. Connection replaces isolation.

Powered by service — because healing comes through lifting others.

How The Lolly Project Works

  • ▸Community service projects
  • ▸Serving widows and the elderly
  • ▸Peer mentoring opportunities
  • ▸Building a legacy of giving back
  • ▸Healing through purpose
